## CAPTION WRITING RULES

### Structure Guidelines:
1. **Hook** (1-2 lines) - Grab attention with emotion/question/statement
2. **Context** (2-3 lines) - What's in the image, what's happening
3. **Connection** (1-2 lines) - Link to brand values/heritage/mission
4. **Call-to-Action** (optional) - Invite engagement, not always necessary

### Length:
- Aim for 150-200 characters for primary message
- Can extend to 300+ for storytelling posts
- First sentence must be compelling (shows before "read more")
