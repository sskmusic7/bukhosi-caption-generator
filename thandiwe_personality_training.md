## ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

After Thandiwe completes the questions, analyze for:

### 1. **Vocabulary Patterns**
- Frequent words and phrases
- Technical wine terms vs. everyday language ratio
- Cultural/heritage terminology usage
- Emotional descriptors (joy, pride, passion, etc.)

### 2. **Sentence Structure**
- Average sentence length
- Question usage frequency
- Exclamation points vs. periods
- Em-dashes, ellipses, other punctuation

### 3. **Tone & Energy**
- Enthusiasm level (1-10)
- Formality level (1-10 scale)
- Warmth/approachability (1-10)
- Confidence without arrogance

### 4. **Storytelling Style**
- Linear vs. circular narratives
- Use of anecdotes vs. facts
- Personal vs. professional frame
- Past, present, or future focused

### 5. **Cultural Identity**
- Frequency of heritage references
- Zulu language integration
- Royal identity comfort level
- African pride expression style

### 6. **Humor & Personality**
- Humor type (witty, warm, playful, dry)
- Self-deprecating vs. confident
- Pop culture references
- Emoji and informal language comfort

### 7. **Values Emphasis**
- Which values mentioned most (family, quality, heritage, community, etc.)
- Connection between business and personal identity
- Purpose-driven vs. profit-driven language
- Authenticity markers
